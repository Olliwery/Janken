About Us

Welcome to Janken, where the timeless game of rock, paper, scissors meets modern innovation and endless fun! Founded by Yong and Ody, Janken brings a fresh twist to this classic game, combining simplicity with excitement for players of all ages.

At Janken, we believe in the power of play. Whether you're looking to unwind after a long day, challenge your friends to a quick match, or simply inject some spontaneity into your routine, our platform is designed to deliver joy and entertainment at your fingertips.

Yong and Ody, the passionate minds behind Janken, envisioned a space where people could come together, connect, and embrace the thrill of competition in its simplest form. With their shared love for gaming and a commitment to creating memorable experiences, they embarked on a journey to bring Janken to life.

What sets Janken apart is not just the game itself, but the community it fosters. We're more than just a platform for playing rock, paper, scissors – we're a hub where friendships are formed, rivalries are born, and laughter is abundant. Whether you're a seasoned strategist or a casual player, there's a place for you here at Janken.

Join us as we embark on this adventure, one throw at a time. Experience the nostalgia of childhood games reimagined for the digital age. Discover the thrill of victory and the camaraderie of competition. Welcome to Janken – where the spirit of play lives on, and every match is a chance to create unforgettable memories.
